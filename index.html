Opening Monero to the world

Haveno will be a non-custodial, decentralized exchange for crypto and national currencies built on Tor and Monero.

Cannot get more private than that.

built on Tor

All connections to Haveno run through Tor. Your private information are safe.

built on monero

Monero makes transactions on Haveno fast, cheap and completely private.

non-custodial

You are the only person in total control of your fund on Haveno.

Easy to use

Everyone should be able to use a P2P exchange, not only power users.

Development and Bounties

Haveno will be composed by a user interface (in ReactJS) and a core app (In Java). The two will communicate through gRPC APIs.

Development is incentivized through bounties, that are awarded in XMR. See the docs page on GitHub.
